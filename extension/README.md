# Growth Signals — Chrome Extension

Instant GTM intelligence for any company you're viewing.

## Install (unpacked)

1. Open `chrome://extensions`
2. Toggle **Developer mode** (top right)
3. Click **Load unpacked** and select this `extension/` folder
4. Click the Growth Signals icon, open **Settings** (⚙), and set the **API URL**
   to your deployed app (e.g. `https://dtcgrowthbenchmark.vercel.app`)

## Use

- Visit a company website (or Shopify store / LinkedIn company page)
- Click the extension — it detects the domain and shows:
  Growth Score, Growth Momentum, Revenue Range, Meta/Google/LinkedIn ad
  activity, top campaign themes, and a Research Brief summary
- If the company hasn't been analyzed yet it shows "Analyzing company…" and
  refreshes automatically when done
- Actions: **Open Full Report**, **Save Company**, **+ Watchlist**
  (Prospects/Clients/Competitors), **Research Brief** (copy)

For LinkedIn/social pages where the domain can't be auto-detected, just type
the company's domain into the box.
